Our Hou Plus mod was initially released 2024/02/07

However, it was updated on 2025-08-17 to add Original/Ryukishi Sprites and Backgrounds

Even if you are not using this mode, please be on the lookout for any graphical bugs which may have been introduced during this update.

Please provide feedback and bug reports on the Hou Plus Github Issues Page, or on our Discord in the #higu_support channel


For details and screenshots on what the mod contains, see the Intro page on our Wiki

  • Full voiceover
  • In-game Background Options

    • Original Japanese release Backgrounds

    • PS3 sprites with lipsync in 1440p
    • Updated MangaGamer sprites in 1440p
    • Original Japanese release sprites
  • Upgraded user interface
  • Opening videos
  • In-game switchable Background Music and Sound Effects (BGM/SE):
    • We highly recommend checking out our BGM/SE FAQ
    • New BGM/SE - The BGM/SE introduced in the April 2019 update and/or Hou+ (vs. the non-plus version)
